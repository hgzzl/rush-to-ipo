QUICK START

Build a durable engine to reach 20.

  1. 01
    Start with $10 and a founder.

    Your founder gives one permanent skill. On each turn, draft from the talent market or complete one open brief.

  2. 02
    Choose how you scale.

    Agencies and staff provide 2–3 skill points but cost $1 each at the start of every turn. Shopify tools provide one permanent skill with no upkeep.

  3. 03
    Pay—or make cuts.

    At the start of your turn, pay the full upkeep or discard agencies and staff until you can afford it.

  4. 04
    Borrow carefully.

    A loan gives you $5 immediately but creates $8 of debt. Repaying $8 uses your turn. You cannot win while any loan remains.

  5. 05
    Your engine is permanent.

    Completing orders and milestones earns cash and reputation without discarding cards. Briefs require 3–8 total skill points, split across the listed skills.

  6. 06
    Win with 20 reputation and no debt.

    Solo games finish immediately. Multiplayer games finish the current round, play one final round, and award the win to the most reputable debt-free company.

  7. 07
    Play against the Chaos Monkey solo.

    After every solo turn, the monkey takes one talent card. It has a 1-in-5 chance to steal an open brief instead. Optional event cards add even more chaos.
